## Key Arguments

- AI affects the Australian labour market primarily through task-level restructuring rather than wholesale job elimination, with different effects across sectors including healthcare, finance, retail, logistics and customer service.
- Wage and employment effects are uneven, with low-wage, female and regional workers facing the greatest adjustment burden.
- The recommended policy package — an Integrated AI and Work Strategy — combines a national reskilling fund, an employer levy, and a Fair Work Act consultation requirement to ensure AI's productivity gains are broadly shared.
